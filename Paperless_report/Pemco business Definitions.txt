Report Name: Paper report

The Paper report dashboard shows a breakdown of the policy counts that are not paperless. It is broken out by if the policy has a self-service account or not. 

Data source -- This dashboard uses the BIM Reporting Weekly files in the edw-prod.pemcoins.net location.

Head of household -- relies on the indicator if the client associated with the paperless setting is the head of the household.

Line of business -- relies on Form Code to determine. 
Self-service account -- This is the self-service account indicator, relying on completion and verification of the self-service account.

Household count -- A distinct count of Household IDs.

Percent -- Calculates the percent of households with at least one completed, verified online account.

Households with unverified email accounts -- This is a count of households that have started the signup process for an online account but have not verified the associated email address.

Household with online account -- This is a count of distinct household id, with an associated complete self-service account. This requires verified email and acceptance of terms and conditions.

Report Name:Online accounts - households and paperless

The Online accounts per Household dashboard shows the current household count, the percent of adoption of self-service by year, if the email has been verified, and the percent of paperless households.

The Household and paperless demographics dashboard has the breakdown of paperless by line of business, the age of the insureds who have self-service accounts, and paperless by form type.  

Data source -- These dashboards rely on the BIM_REPORTING_WEEKLY in the EDW environment. 

Data update time -- A extract at 6 a.m., reliant on the weekly upload of the BIM data.

Household -- looks at unique household ID number, made up of multiple policies. 

Paperless -- to be counted as paperless, the policy must be in force, the self-service account activated, and the email address verified. The policy must also qualify for paperless, i.e. a homeowners, with a "paperless bill" setting but with a mortgagee, won't qualify.

Self-service account -- The account must be completed and verified (the email address is verified).

Report Name: Online_Accounts

This dashboard was built with the purpose of showing the difference in counts based on date of creation of account versus date of activation.

The date from this comes from the ASP Membership database and connects with Active Directory for the processor information. 

Authored by Kelly Kurttila

Activation date -- The date the self-service account is completed with acceptance of Terms & Conditions in addition to verification of email address.

Creation date -- The date the new self-service account was created, or started - not requiring completion of account set-up. 

Gross count -- The count of new online accounts during a time frame, based on activation date, and regardless of policy status. This allows for counting new online accounts that may now be in a cancelled state. 

The Account by LOB/State tab shows results based on the following:
1) The numbers are currently active accounts (either for NI or ANI) that have an inforce policy.
2)Primary state is derived from the policy rating risk state.
3) If a client has multiple policies, the online account is counted in each line of business.

Report Name: Paperless Online accounts 

This dashboard is intended for subscription and was originally created for maintenance of divi numbers at the request of Accounting and Digital Services. The supporting worksheets within the workbook are intended for deeper dives into the demographics and background information around paperless and online accounts.

Data source - the dashboard relies on BIM_Reporting_Weekly for householding information and for the paperless and account information. For inforce policy count, it is utilizing the vw_policy in the edw-prod warehouse.

Paperless - at the policy level, the online account has been completed and the customer has selected either to be paperless billing, paperless policy or both. The email must be verified, and if paperless billing is selected, there cannot be a lien holder or mortgagee.

Online account - the setup of the onlie account must be completed, terms and conditions accepted, email verified in order to be counted. 

Unverified email - an email has been sent to customer, but a response from the email address has not been received, verifying the existence of the address. 

Gross count - does not adjust over time, the actual count of paperless or account setups at the time, does not remove policies when they cancel later.

Net count - adjusts the history numbers based on policy status, i.e. inforce/cancels.
