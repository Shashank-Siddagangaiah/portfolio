PRIORITY #1 BEFORE DAY 1 — Get a Blood Test
Menstruating women lose iron every month. Iron deficiency (ferritin below 50 ng/mL) is the #1 missed cause of fatigue, poor recovery, brain fog, and weight-loss resistance in active women. You can train perfectly and eat well — and none of it will work if your ferritin is at 15–20 ng/mL (extremely common in India). Test: Ferritin · Vitamin D (25-OH) · Thyroid (TSH, T3, T4) · Hemoglobin · Fasting glucose. Do this before Week 1. Thyroid issues are 5–8× more common in women and directly mimic weight-loss resistance.

Creatine — Why Women Benefit MORE Than Men
2025 research (Journal of the International Society of Sports Nutrition): women have 70–80% lower endogenous creatine stores than men. This means women have far more "room to fill" — greater response per gram supplemented. Benefits confirmed: strength, power, workout recovery, AND cognitive function (focus, reaction time, mood). Start 3–5g/day immediately — no loading phase needed. One of the most underused supplements in women's fitness.

🚶 NEAT — Your Biggest Fat Loss Lever Outside the Gym

📊
Research fact: NEAT (Non-Exercise Activity Thermogenesis — everything you do outside structured workouts) varies up to 2,000 kcal/day between people of the same size. Two people eating the same and doing the same gym sessions can have dramatically different results purely from NEAT. For sustainable fat loss, this is often more impactful than adding a 4th workout.
👟

8,000–10,000 Steps Daily

Non-training days: 8,000 minimum. Training days: 10,000. App: Google Fit or iPhone Health. Step count is the single most trackable NEAT metric.

🚶

10 min Walk After Each Meal

3 walks × 10 min = 30 min daily = ~200 kcal. Also measurably reduces blood sugar spikes after meals, improving fat burning and energy levels.

🏢

Stairs Always, Never Lift

Every stair session burns 8–10 kcal. Over 4 months — hundreds of extra kcal. Non-negotiable daily habit with zero extra time cost.

📱

Stand During Phone Calls

Standing burns 50% more calories than sitting. An hour of calls = 50 extra kcal. Walk around during long calls — adds 100–150 kcal/hr.
